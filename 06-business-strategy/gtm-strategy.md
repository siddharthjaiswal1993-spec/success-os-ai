# Go-to-Market Strategy — SuccessOS AI

---

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

**Primary ICP:**
- B2B SaaS company
- $5M–$100M ARR
- 10–150 CSM headcount
- CS team currently manages accounts manually (spreadsheets, CRM notes, tribal knowledge)
- Company has experienced at least one surprise churn event in the last 12 months
- CS Director or VP CS owns the budget

**Signals that indicate ICP fit:**
- Job posts for "CS Operations" or "CS Enablement" roles
- Recent round of funding (Series B–D)
- CRM is Salesforce or HubSpot (integration ready)
- CS team size 10–60 (sweet spot for initial deployment)
- Company has grown CS team by > 30% in last 18 months (scaling pain)

**ICP Exclusions (do not pursue):**
- Consumer companies (B2C — wrong motion)
- Single-product companies with < 100 customers (not enough scale)
- Companies without dedicated CS function (premature)
- Companies with CS teams of < 5 (insufficient volume for AI)

---

## Sales Motion

### Phase 1: Land with a Champion (0–6 months)

Target: CS Directors and VP CS at ICP accounts.

Motion:
1. Outbound: LinkedIn + email sequence to CS Director/VP CS; opening angle is churn recovery cost, not AI features
2. Discovery: focus on how they currently detect churn risk; map the manual work
3. Demo: Personalized demo using their industry (e.g., FinTech, HealthTech) as context
4. Pilot: 30-day paid pilot with 1 CSM team; target 1 real QBR and 1 real churn risk catch
5. Expansion: Pilot success → full team deployment

Champion persona: CS Director who owns churn numbers and is frustrated by manual processes. They need a win to show their CEO.

### Phase 2: Expand Within Accounts (6–18 months)

After initial deployment, expand:
- From 1 CSM team → full CS department
- From Starter tier → Growth tier
- From Growth → Enterprise with integrations

Expansion trigger: Any pilot team that achieves ≥ 2 hours/CSM/week saved AND 1 documented churn save in 90 days is a strong expansion candidate.

### Phase 3: Build the Category (18+ months)

Invest in community, content, and brand to become the category-defining platform for AI-native CS:
- CS Leader roundtables
- Annual benchmark report: "State of AI in Customer Success"
- Certification program for CS teams using the platform
- Partner program with CS consulting firms

## Marketing Strategy

**Primary Channel:** Content Marketing + Organic

Target keywords: "churn prediction software," "customer success AI," "QBR automation," "AI customer success platform"

Content types:
- Benchmark data: churn rates by industry, segment, and CS team size
- Playbooks: "How to detect churn before the renewal conversation"
- Case studies: anonymized, metric-led outcome stories
- Product explainer videos: 3-minute walkthroughs of specific use cases

**Secondary Channel:** LinkedIn (Paid + Organic)

Target audiences: CS Directors, VP CS, CS Operations, Revenue Operations

Content angle: ROI-driven — "What if your team caught 40% more churn risk signals?"

**Tertiary Channel:** CS Community Partnerships

Sponsorship and integration with CS community platforms, newsletters, and events. Target communities where CS professionals gather to discuss tools and practices.
