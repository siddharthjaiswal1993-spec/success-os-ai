# Pricing Strategy — SuccessOS AI

---

## Pricing Philosophy

1. **Price on value, not inputs.** We do not charge per AI call or per QBR generated. We charge per CSM seat — aligned with how buyers budget for software.

2. **Make the decision easy at the bottom.** The Starter tier should be easy to approve without procurement. $49/seat × 10 seats = $490/month — a line item, not a budget cycle.

3. **Expansion should be natural.** Moving from Starter to Growth should feel like unlocking more of the same product, not buying a new product.

4. **Enterprise pricing reflects partnership.** At the Enterprise tier, we're not just selling software — we're selling AI infrastructure for a CS organization. Custom pricing reflects custom complexity.

---

## Tier Definitions

### Starter — $49/CSM seat/month (billed annually)

**Who it's for:** Series A–B companies; CS teams of 5–20; testing AI in CS for the first time

**What's included:**
- Meeting Intelligence (up to 20 analyses/seat/month)
- Account Health Dashboard (manual signal entry + AI-computed score)
- Basic Recommended Actions (3 action types: check-in, internal escalation, monitor)
- QBR Generator (up to 5 accounts/month)
- Email/Slack weekly digest
- Slack integration only

**Limits:**
- Up to 200 managed accounts per workspace
- 90 days of meeting history
- 1 CRM integration (read-only)

---

### Growth — $89/CSM seat/month (billed annually)

**Who it's for:** Series B–D; CS teams of 20–100; ready to systematize AI-assisted CS

**What's included (everything in Starter, plus):**
- Meeting Intelligence (unlimited analyses)
- Feature Impact Agent (up to 5 feature analyses/month)
- Full Recommended Actions (all 6 action types)
- QBR Generator (unlimited)
- Expansion Signal tracking with AI scoring
- CRM integration: read + write (activity logging)
- API access (read-only)
- Advanced analytics: cohort analysis, CSM performance, churn prediction accuracy
- Up to 2,000 managed accounts
- 18 months of meeting history

---

### Enterprise — $149/CSM seat/month base + custom (negotiated annually)

**Who it's for:** Late-stage / public companies; 100+ CSM teams; enterprise procurement cycles

**What's included (everything in Growth, plus):**
- Unlimited accounts and history
- Custom agentic workflows (Professional Services)
- Multi-workspace and team segmentation
- SAML SSO and advanced RBAC
- Custom CRM integrations
- Priority support (4-hour response SLA)
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager
- Quarterly business review with SuccessOS AI product team
- Data export API (full account data)
- Custom AI model fine-tuning (Volume 500+ seats)
- Platform API (embed signals into external BI tools)

## Competitive Positioning on Price

- Traditional CS platforms (legacy): $80–$150/seat — similar price, but limited AI intelligence
- AI point solutions (churn prediction only): $20–$40/seat — cheaper but narrow
- SuccessOS AI: $49–$149/seat — full signal-to-action platform, not a point solution

We are positioned as a premium alternative to legacy platforms at comparable or lower price, with significantly higher value because the AI layer replaces manual work across the CS workflow.

## Discounting Policy

| Situation | Allowable Discount | Approver |
|-----------|-------------------|---------|
| Annual vs. monthly | 15–20% (standard) | Automatic |
| Multi-year contract (2yr+) | Up to 25% | VP Sales |
| Large volume (50+ seats) | Up to 20% | AE discretion |
| Competitive displacement | Up to 25% for Year 1 | VP Sales |
| Non-profit / education | Up to 30% | CEO approval |

No discounting below $35/seat/month (floor) without CEO approval.
